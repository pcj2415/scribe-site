Questions, answered.

What markets does Scribe support?

All of them — that's the point. Scribe is built for the modern trader: stocks, options, crypto, forex, and prediction markets (like Kalshi event contracts) are all first-class citizens. Whether you're swing trading equities, selling options, or trading event outcomes, everything lives in one journal.

How does the AI coaching work?

Scribe's AI has full context on your trading history — every trade, every account, every pattern. Ask it anything: "When do I trade best?", "Compare my IRA vs my prediction account", or run an instant AI Trade Review on any closed trade for sharp, specific feedback on your entry, exit, sizing, and risk. It can even search the web for market context.

Can I import my existing trades?

Yes — Scribe Pro imports stock and options trades via CSV from major brokers including Robinhood, Charles Schwab / thinkorswim, Webull, and Fidelity, with a preview step so you can confirm everything before it's added. And logging any trade manually takes just seconds.

Is my trading data private?

Completely. Scribe is a private journal — your data is encrypted, belongs to you, and is never sold or shared with advertisers. Scribe doesn't connect to your brokerage accounts or hold any credentials; you're always in control of what you log.

Is there a free version?

Yes. Scribe is free to download and use, with up to 5 journal entries per day and core performance metrics. Scribe Pro unlocks unlimited journaling, AI coaching and trade reviews, advanced metrics with your Trader Score, trade importing, and multiple accounts — with a 7-day free trial so you can experience everything before paying a cent.

Does Scribe give financial advice?

No — and that's by design. Scribe analyzes what you've already done to help you understand your own patterns and improve your process. It's AI-powered analysis of your trading, not financial advice or buy/sell recommendations. Your strategy stays yours.
